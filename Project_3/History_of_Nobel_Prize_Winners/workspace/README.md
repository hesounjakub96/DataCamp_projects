#  Visualizing the History of Nobel Prize Winners

##  Objective
Analyze Nobel Prize winner data to identify patterns in gender distribution, country of origin, and historical trends across decades and categories.

##  Analysis
- Identified the most common gender and country among winners
- Analyzed distribution of US-born winners across decades
- Examined female ratio over time
- Found the first female Nobel Prize winner and her category
- Identified individuals/organizations with multiple Nobel Prizes
